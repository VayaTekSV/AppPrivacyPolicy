Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

VayaTek ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application Calculator Credentials Vault - Secure Credentials Manager ("App").

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

The App is designed with privacy as a core principle and does not collect, transmit, store, or share your personal information on external servers.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Credentials, passwords, notes, and other information entered by you into the secure vault.
  • A phone number provided by you for:
    • Password recovery via One-Time Password (OTP).
    • Security alerts related to unauthorized access attempts.

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to VayaTek or any third party.

How We Use Information

The information stored locally is used solely to provide the App's functionality, including:

  • Secure storage of credentials and sensitive information.
  • Password recovery through SMS-based OTP.
  • Security alerts for unauthorized access attempts.
  • User authentication and access management.

Data Storage and Security

The App is designed to keep user data exclusively on the device.

Security measures include:

  • AES-256 encryption for protecting stored data.
  • PIN and password protection.
  • Biometric authentication support (where available on the device).
  • Automatic logout after periods of inactivity.
  • Screenshot blocking to help protect sensitive information.
  • Security alerts for repeated unauthorized access attempts.

Although we implement security measures to protect information, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

No Cloud Storage

The App does not:

  • Upload your data to cloud servers.
  • Synchronize data with online accounts.
  • Maintain copies of your credentials on external systems.
  • Operate backend servers for storing user data.

All vault information remains on the user's device.

SMS Permissions

The App may request SMS-related permissions solely for features such as:

  • Sending or managing password recovery OTPs.
  • Delivering security-related alerts configured by the user.

These permissions are used only for the intended functionality of the App.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user information with third parties.

Because user data remains on the device, VayaTek generally does not have access to the information stored within the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Marketing platforms
  • Third-party data collection services

No third-party services are used to collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App is a utility application intended for general audiences and is not specifically directed toward children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users through external servers or online services.

Data Retention

All information remains stored locally on the user's device until:

  • The user deletes the information.
  • The application data is cleared.
  • The application is uninstalled.

When the App is uninstalled, locally stored App data is removed from the device according to the operating system's data management processes.

User Rights

Since user information is stored locally on the device and not on VayaTek servers:

  • Users maintain direct control over their data.
  • Users may modify or delete stored information at any time.
  • Users may uninstall the App to remove stored App data from their device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
