Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

VayaTek ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application Calculator Credentials Vault - Secure Credentials Manager ("App").

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data remains under your control.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information on external servers.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Credentials, passwords, notes, and vault data entered by you.
  • An optional phone number provided by you for security purposes. This is stored locally using device storage and is not transmitted anywhere.

Providing a phone number is completely optional and depends on user choice.

How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used only for app functionality:

  • Secure storage of credentials and sensitive data.
  • Password recovery via SMS-based OTP (when user initiates recovery).
  • Security alerts triggered after repeated invalid login attempts.
  • User authentication and access control.

SMS Permissions

The App uses SMS functionality only in specific, user-initiated security scenarios:

  • Password recovery when the user requests OTP verification.
  • Security alerts triggered after repeated invalid password or PIN attempts.

SMS messages are initiated only from the user’s device and only to the user’s registered recovery number. No SMS content, logs, or metadata are stored or shared externally.

No SMS activity occurs in the background or without explicit user action.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored exclusively on the user’s device.

  • AES-256 encryption for protecting stored data.
  • PIN and password protection.
  • Biometric authentication support (if available on device).
  • Automatic logout after inactivity.
  • Screenshot blocking for privacy protection.
  • Intruder detection for repeated failed login attempts.

No Cloud Storage

The App does not:

  • Upload data to any server or cloud platform.
  • Synchronize data across devices.
  • Use backend services for storing user data.

All information remains fully on the user’s device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

Since all data remains on the device, VayaTek does not have access to user vault data or phone numbers.

Third-Party Services

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking or marketing SDKs
  • No third-party data collection tools

Children's Privacy

The App is a general utility application and is not specifically directed toward children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through external systems or servers.

Data Retention

  • Data remains stored on the device until deleted by the user.
  • Data is removed when the app is uninstalled or app data is cleared.

User Rights

  • Users fully control their data.
  • Users can modify or delete stored data anytime.
  • Users may uninstall the app to remove all local data.
